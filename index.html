How to Play

Welcome to ____ipedia! The goal is to guess the Wikipedia article.

  • You will be shown the first paragraph of a Wikipedia article, with the title and key words blanked out.
  • You will also see a list of categories the article belongs to.
  • Type your guess into the box and hit "Guess".
  • If you're right, you'll earn a point and can try the "Down the Rabbit Hole" mode, where the next article is a link from the one you just guessed!
  • If you're wrong, your streak resets.

Settings

Down the Rabbit Hole Mode

When enabled, the next puzzle will be an article linked from the one you just guessed, allowing you to follow a path of related topics.
